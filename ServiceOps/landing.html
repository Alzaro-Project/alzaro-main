Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026

Summary: We collect only what we need to provide the service. We don't sell your data. Your business information stays yours. We use industry-standard security.

1. Who we are

Alzaro ServiceOps ("we", "us", "our") is a job management software service for tradespeople, operated by Alzaro Group, based in Milton Keynes, United Kingdom.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

2. What data we collect

Account information

  • Name and email address
  • Business name and address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)

Business data you enter

  • Customer information
  • Quotes and invoices
  • Job schedules and records
  • Materials and pricing

Technical data

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns within the application

3. How we use your data

  • Provide and maintain the ServiceOps service
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send important service updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our software
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Ever.

We only share data with:

  • Stripe: For payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers: For secure storage
  • Legal authorities: Only when required by law

5. Data security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Daily backups

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent

Contact us at privacy@alzaro.co.uk

7. Data retention

We retain your data while your account is active. After closure:

  • 30 days to export your data
  • Deletion within 90 days
  • Some data retained for legal compliance

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for login and preferences. No advertising or tracking cookies.

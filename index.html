Alzaro TyreOps

Drive your tyre business forward

TyreOps is a purpose-built tyre garage management platform designed specifically for UK tyre retailers. It combines FIFO batch inventory tracking, instant invoicing, VAT margin scheme calculations, and a full customer database — all in one place. Whether you're a single-bay operation or a multi-location garage, TyreOps gives you the tools to run a more profitable, more organised business.

About Alzaro

We don't do everything.
We do five things exceptionally.

Alzaro was built on a simple idea — that a specialist always beats a generalist. So instead of one company that does everything average, we built five platforms that each do one thing at the highest level.

Specialist Focus

Every Alzaro platform exists for one vertical only. No distractions, no compromises — just deep expertise in a single domain.

Built from Scratch

Each platform is purpose-built, not retrofitted. We design from the ground up to fit the real needs of the people using it.

UK Rooted

Based in the UK, built for the UK. We understand the local market, the regulations, and the people we serve.

One Standard

Five brands, one benchmark. Every Alzaro product is held to the same bar — professional, reliable, and honest.

Built to Scale

People First

Our Story

Started with one idea.
Grew into five.

Alzaro started with a simple observation: the tools available to small businesses in automotive and trades were too generic, too expensive, or not built for the UK market.

So we built TyreOps. Then GarageOps. Then we kept going — into servicing, solo traders, and property. Each platform came from a real gap and a determination to fill it properly.

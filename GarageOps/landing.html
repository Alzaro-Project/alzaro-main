BAY 01

Job cards

Raise a job in seconds, add services and parts, track status from booked-in to collected. Every job, every vehicle, on record.

BAY 02

Bookings calendar

Month, week and day views with colour-coded statuses. See exactly what's coming through the doors and when.

BAY 03

Services catalogue

Your full menu of services with set prices — add them to jobs in one tap. No more quoting from memory.

BAY 04

Customer records

Sales & reports

Daily, weekly and monthly takings at a glance — plus deeper reporting on Silver and Gold so you know what's actually making money.

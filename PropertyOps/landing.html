How it works

From shoebox to system in an afternoon

Step 01

Add your properties

House, flat, HMO or block — set the address, the type and the rent, and the record is live.

Step 02

Move your tenants in

Attach tenancies to each property. Joint tenancies for houses and flats, room-by-room for HMOs.

Step 03

Let the rent roll

Recurring invoices generate themselves and project forward, so expected income is always visible.

Step 04

Watch the whole picture

Maintenance, compliance and finance feed one dashboard — and one honest P&L per property.

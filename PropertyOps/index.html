Summary: We collect only what we need. We don't sell your data. Your property and tenant information stays yours. Bank-level security.

1. Who we are

Alzaro PropertyOps is property management software for UK landlords, operated by Alzaro Group, Milton Keynes, United Kingdom.

2. What data we collect

  • Account information (name, email, phone)
  • Property details you enter
  • Tenant information (names, contact details, tenancy data)
  • Financial records (rent, expenses)
  • Documents you upload

You are the data controller for tenant information. We process it on your behalf under our data processing agreement.

We do not sell your data. We share only with Stripe (payments) and cloud providers (storage).

5. Security

SSL encryption, encrypted storage, daily backups, regular security audits.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR: access, correct, delete, export your data. Contact: privacy@alzaro.co.uk

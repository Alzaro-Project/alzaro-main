Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026

Summary: We collect only what we need to provide Alzaro TyreOps services. We never sell your data. Your garage data belongs to you.

1. Who we are

Alzaro TyreOps ("we", "us", "our") is a tyre management software platform operated in the United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Contact: privacy@tyreops.co.uk

2. What data we collect

Account information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Garage name and contact details

Business data you enter

  • Inventory records (SKUs, batches, stock levels)
  • Customer information (names, emails, phone numbers, vehicle registrations)
  • Invoice and payment records
  • Supplier information

Automatically collected

  • IP address and browser type
  • Usage analytics (pages visited, features used)
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

3. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain Alzaro TyreOps services
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send service-related emails (password resets, subscription updates)
  • Improve our software based on usage patterns
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records)

4. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, with servers located in the European Union. We implement:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Row-level security ensuring garage data isolation
  • Regular security audits

5. Data sharing

We never sell your data. We only share data with:

  • Supabase — database hosting (EU servers)
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — application hosting

All third parties are GDPR-compliant and process data only as necessary to provide their services.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Portability — export your data in a standard format
  • Object — opt out of certain processing

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@tyreops.co.uk.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Business data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days
  • Anonymised analytics may be retained indefinitely

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only:

  • Authentication cookies — to keep you logged in
  • Session cookies — to maintain your session state

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@tyreops.co.uk
Address: Alzaro TyreOps, Milton Keynes, United Kingdom

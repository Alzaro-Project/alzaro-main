The stock engine

The clever bits, running quietly underneath

Two things tyre garages get wrong in spreadsheets — priced batches and part-worn VAT — handled properly, automatically.

FIFO automatic pricing

Bought the same tyre at three different prices? TyreOps sells the oldest batch first and margins stay true — with batch override control on Silver+ when you need to take charge.

Batch 04 Mar · 8 units @ £61.20← selling
Batch 22 Apr · 12 units @ £64.80queued

VAT margin scheme

Part-worn tyres bought from the public are VAT-margin sales — VAT due on the margin, not the full price. TyreOps calculates it per sale and rolls it into your VAT reports on Gold.
